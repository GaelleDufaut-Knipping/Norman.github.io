WHO HE IS ?

Born on December 25, 1935, he received his doctorate in cognitive psychology from the University of Pennsylvania in 1962. Norman worked in product design and ergonomics for many years.

He is well-known for his contributions to user-centered design and ergonomics. Don Norman has held important jobsat major companies like Apple, Hewlett-Packard, and others in the technology industry. Additionally, he served as a professor of cognitive psychology and communication sciences at the University of California, San Diego.

This book has become a classic in the fields of design and ergonomics, important valuable insights into the intuitive and user-friendly design of everyday objects.
